# 🎬 Social Media Video Downloader

A fast, simple, and open-source tool to grab **public videos** from all your favorite platforms — including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, X and more. Whether you're a techie or a casual user, this tool works seamlessly across **Android (Termux)**, **Linux**, and **Windows**.

Download single videos or entire batches, pick your desired format, and save content hassle-free. With a user-friendly command-line interface, you can easily download videos in just a few clicks.
